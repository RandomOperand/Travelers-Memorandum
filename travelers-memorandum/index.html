About the Blog

As you might've seen in the README.md, the Traveler's Memorandum is a small, personal project I've endeavored in to improve my Japanese output, and provide ample motivation for my journey, as well as your own.

Since around 1/6/2025, I began learning Japanese through the Kaishi 1.5k deck, initially at 10 card reviews per day before increasing that amount to 22 as my recognition of word structure grew. Since 4/2/2025 (indeed, the day prior to the blog's development), I have finished the Kaishi deck and have moved onto sentence mining and immersion. As immersion has become my primary goal, I have opted to create the blog you are currently reading. I'll occasionally share things that I find interesting (perhaps the occassional analysis or grammar point), along with posts regarding various pieces of media in some form; as of now, a Log Horizon manga chapter-by-chapter review is planned.
